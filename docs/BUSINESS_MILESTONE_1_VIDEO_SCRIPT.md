# Business Milestone 1 Animated Video Script

## 操作

推荐形式：

- 用 PowerPoint 或 Google Slides 录制
- 做成 6 页左右的简洁动画视频
- 每页字不要太多
- 用大标题 + 图片 + 简单动画
- 动画建议只用 `Fade`、`Appear`、`Zoom`

整体风格建议：

- 面向普通人，不要讲技术术语
- 让爷爷奶奶也能听懂
- 强调“事故后很慌张，ClaimMate 可以帮助用户”
- 不要讲 RAG、embedding、vector store 这些词

建议时长：

- 60 到 90 秒

---

### Slide 1: Hook

页面标题建议：

`A Car Accident Takes Seconds. The Stress Can Last Weeks.`

画面建议：

- 一张车祸或车辆碰撞的图片
- 再依次出现：
  - 手机图标
  - 文件图标
  - 日历图标

你录的时候：

- 先停 1 秒让标题出来
- 再让图片和图标逐个出现
- 这一页不要切太快

---

### Slide 2: The Problem

页面标题建议：

`Insurance Is Hard to Understand When You Need It Most`

画面建议：

- 一个困惑的车主
- 一份保单文件
- 一个 deadline 日历
- 两三个聊天气泡，代表保险公司、修车厂、理赔沟通

你录的时候：

- 先出现标题
- 再让这些元素一个个弹出来
- 让画面表达“信息很多，但人很混乱”

---

### Slide 3: The Solution

页面标题建议：

`Meet ClaimMate`

副标题建议：

`An AI Copilot for Car Insurance Claims`

画面建议：

- 产品名字 `ClaimMate`
- 一张手机或网页 mockup
- 一个比较温和、可信赖的配色

你录的时候：

- 这一页要像“产品正式出场”
- 标题可以单独先出来，再出现副标题和产品 mockup

---

### Slide 4: How It Helps

页面标题建议：

`Upload. Ask. Understand.`

画面建议：

- 三个横向步骤
  - Upload policy
  - Ask a question
  - Get a clear answer
- 每一步配一个简单图标

你录的时候：

- 三步最好按顺序出现
- 让观众觉得这个产品使用门槛很低

---

### Slide 5: Why It Matters

页面标题建议：

`Better Information Can Protect Real People`

画面建议：

- 家庭或普通车主的温和图片
- 钱的图标
- 盾牌或 check 图标

你录的时候：

- 这一页的语气要从“产品功能”转到“现实意义”
- 不要讲太技术，讲人的压力、时间、金钱、决策

---

### Slide 6: Milestone 1

页面标题建议：

`Milestone 1: AI Core in Progress`

副标题建议：

`Building the foundation for smarter claims support`

画面建议：

- 一个很简单的 roadmap
  - AI Core
  - Full Workflow
  - Complete Product
- 把第一个阶段高亮

你录的时候：

- 强调这是第一阶段
- 已经有工作成果
- 后面还会继续扩展成更完整的产品

---

## Script

An accident can happen in just a few seconds. But the confusion after it can last for weeks.

People suddenly have to deal with insurance documents, deadlines, claim rules, and stressful conversations, often at the exact moment they feel the most overwhelmed.

Most drivers are not insurance experts. They do not know how to read a long policy document, track claim deadlines, or figure out what to say after an accident.

A simple mistake or a missed deadline can cost time, money, and peace of mind.

That is why we built ClaimMate.

ClaimMate is an AI-powered copilot for car insurance claims. Its goal is to help ordinary people understand their coverage, stay on top of important deadlines, and feel supported during the claims process.

With ClaimMate, a user can upload their policy, ask questions in plain English, and receive clear answers based on real policy and regulatory sources.

Instead of digging through confusing documents alone, they get direct and understandable support.

This matters because insurance confusion is not just inconvenient. It can have real consequences.

If people better understand their policy and deadlines, they are more likely to protect their rights, avoid costly mistakes, and make better decisions after an accident.

In Milestone 1, we are building the AI foundation of ClaimMate.

We already have a working backend that can ingest policy documents, answer grounded questions, and support repeatable demos. This is the first step toward a much more complete claims support experience.

ClaimMate is about making insurance less confusing, less stressful, and more human.
